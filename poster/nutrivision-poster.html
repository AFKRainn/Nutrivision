1. Problem description

Many users struggle to decide what to cook from the ingredients already available at home. At the same time, manual calorie tracking is tedious, beginner cooks need clear guidance, and forgotten ingredients often expire before being used. NutriVision addresses these daily problems by combining fridge image recognition, personalized meal suggestions, and simple nutrition tracking inside Telegram.

2. The Solution
Telegramuser sends photos
YOLOv12detects ingredients

The assistant turns a short Telegram interaction into a complete cooking flow: detect ingredients, correct the list, receive three diet-aware meals, choose one, and save the result for future analytics.
